Privacy Policy

App: OTG USB Free (it.adaptit.otgusbfree)
Publisher: AdaptIT
Last updated: May 2, 2026

1. Overview

OTG USB Free is an Android application that displays the live video feed from a USB UVC camera connected to the Android device via an OTG cable. This privacy policy describes how the app handles data and permissions.

2. Data we collect

The app does not collect, store or transmit any personal data.

  • The app does not use user accounts.
  • The app does not establish any internet connection and does not send data to any remote server.
  • The app does not use analytics services, advertising trackers, or third-party SDKs that collect data.
  • The app does not share any information with third parties.

3. Required permissions

Camera (android.permission.CAMERA)

This permission is required by Android to allow the app to access live video streams, including the stream coming from the external USB UVC camera connected via OTG. The video stream is shown only on the device screen and is not automatically recorded, stored or transmitted.

USB Host (android.hardware.usb.host)

Required to communicate with the USB camera that is physically connected to the device via OTG cable.

4. Snapshots

The user can manually save a JPEG snapshot of the current image by tapping the dedicated button. The image is saved only on the local device, in the Pictures/USBCameraViewer folder, using the standard Android MediaStore API. The app does not access other images on the device and does not transmit snapshots to any third party.

5. Children

The app is not specifically directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

6. Security

Because no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of personal data breach caused by the app.

7. Changes

Any changes to this policy will be published at this same URL, with the date at the top of the page updated accordingly.
